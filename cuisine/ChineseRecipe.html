AboutA complete dish with rice,vegetables and egg!!

Ingredients


For Chinese Fried Rice

  • 3 cup cooked rice(for exact Bangladeshi style use kalijeera or chinigura)
  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter/ ghee
  • 2 cup your choice vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, cauliflower, tomato, cabbage, capsicum, bay corn etc.)
  • 3-4 eggs (large)
  • 1/3 cup onion sliced
  • 1/2 cup spring onion chopped
  • 2-3 green chillies chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon sugar or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon tasting salt to taste(optional)
  • Salt to taste

Direction


Processing:

Related Video
Step 1:Cut vegetables into your desire size sliced or diced. Beat eggs with salt; Add 1 tablespoon oil in pan, add eggs in pan and scramble it (or you can fry eggs and cut into pieces late but donâ€™t make it hard, softer test better). Remove the eggs from pan and same pan add rest of oil.
Step 2: When oil is ready, add vegetables and pinch of salt and fry 1-2 minutes. Stir it. Add onion, green chillies and after 1 minute add rice. Stir it and add one by one soy sauce, pepper, salt, tomato sauce, fried eggs, spring onion sugar and tasting salt. Stir well.
Step 3: When all ingredients mix well, add butter / ghee and turn off heat. Enjoy Bangladeshi Chinese Style Egg Fried Rice.



AboutVegetables in a soupy way!!

Ingredients


For Chinese Vegetable

  • Boneless Chicken: 1 cup
  • Black Pepper: 1/2 tsp
  • Soy Sauce 1tsp
  • Lime Juice 1 tsp
  • Carrot, Green Beans, Cauliflower, Zucchini, Broccoli, Yellow and Red Bell Pepper 1/2 cup
  • Garlic 1 tsp
  • Ginger 1 tsp
  • Chicken Stock 1/2 + 1/2 cup
  • Onion 1/4 cup
  • Green Chilli 4 pcs
  • Light Soy Sauce 1tsp
  • Fish Sauce 1 tsp
  • Corn Starch 1 tbsp

Direction


Processing:

Related Video
Step 1:Mix chicken, soy sauce, salt, and black pepper in a bowl and marinate for 1 hr.Half boil broccoli, carrot, zucchini, green beans , cauliflower for 2 min.
Step 2:Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat; Add ginger ,garlic and chicken cook and stir chicken until meat is no longer pink in the center, add half cup of chicken stock to cook about 5-10 minutes. Transfer chicken and sauce to a bowl and wipe skillet clean.
Step 3: Heat remaining 1 tablespoon vegetable oil over medium-high heat; . Stir in onion , green chilli , vegetables; continue to cook add fish oil ,soy sauce and the chicken. Pour remaining chicken stock and 1 tbsp corn starch .
Enjoy the deliciousness!




AboutA saucy curry with crispy chicken!

Ingredients


For Chicken Manchurian

  • Chicken breast - 500 gm
  • Egg - 1 small
  • Salt - to taste
  • White pepper powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Cornstarch - 1/4 cup
  • All purpose flour - 1/2 cup


For Sauce

  • Oil - 2 tablespoon
  • Cashews - 2 tablespoon
  • Ginger - 1 tablespoon finely chopped
  • Garlic - 1 tablespoon finely chopped
  • Onion - 1 cup cut into cube
  • Soy sauce - 2 tablespoon
  • Tomato puree - 1 cup
  • Tomato sauce - 2 tablespoon
  • Cornstarch - 3 tablespoon
  • Red Chili Flakes - 1 tablespoon
  • Green Chilies - 2 cut into julianno
  • Capsicum - 1 cut into cube
  • Vinegar - 4 tablespoon

Direction


Processing:

Related Video
Step 1:Cut chicken breast in to cubes, mix it in egg ,pepper and salt.Keep aside. In a a bowl mix cornstarch and flour together.Coat the chicken cube with flour mixture one by one. Heat oil in a deep fry pan and fry the coated chicken cube until light brown in color.Keep on a kitchen tissue.
Step 2:Pour two tablespoon oil in a another pan,add cashews and fry until golden in color. Add onion,ginger and garlic to saute. Add tomato puree and simmer for a while then add marinated chicken and cook it well then add capsicum, vinegar, soya sauce, red chilies,green chilies, salt, tomato sauce and cashews, mix it well and cook for 2 mins.
Step 3: Dissolve three tablespoon corn flour in water and add into sauce to make a thick gravy. Keep the flame on high. Check the consistency of gravy; now pour the gravy in serving dish and serve it with fried rice.
